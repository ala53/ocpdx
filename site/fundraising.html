Donations

The Optimist Group, including our chapter, runs on the donations of people like you. We know you may not be able to donate much, but any help you can give will be appreciated - time or money.
Even if you can't donate money or time, you can still help by getting the word out for us. Share on facebook or send out a tweet linking back to us.
