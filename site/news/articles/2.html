Candlelighters Barbecue at Oaks Park 06/26/2016 03:17 PM
We just had our yearly barbecue with Candlelighters yesterday. As a fundraiser for the Candlelighters charity, which helps children who have cancer, hundreds came and enjoyed a lunch provided by our chapter of the Optimist Club and rides at Oaks Park. Pictures are available here.
If you haven't heard of Candlelighters, check them out at 4kidswithcancer.org. Feel free to donate to either of us to support our work.
