Yearly Contests

There's the two yearly contests hosted by Optimist international: the Oratorical Contest and the Essay Contest.

Oratorical Contest
The Oratorical contest is a constitutional speech scholarship contest designed to increase awareness of the constitution among high school students under 20, giving out over 138,000 dollars in scholarships each year.
